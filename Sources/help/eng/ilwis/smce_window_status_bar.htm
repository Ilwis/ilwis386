SMCE window

Status bar

A status bar is located at the bottom of the SMCE window. The status bar can be shown or hidden with the Status Bar command on the View menu.

The status bar gives brief explanations on:

  • highlighted menu commands;
  • the functionality of buttons in the toolbar when moving with the mouse pointer over a button;
  • selected items in the criteria tree viewer.
