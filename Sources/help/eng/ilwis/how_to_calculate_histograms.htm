How to calculate a histogram

A histogram can be calculated for any raster, polygon, segment or point map.

To calculate a histogram:

There are several ways to calculate a histogram:

  • from the Operations menu, select Statistics and Histogram,
  • open the Statistics item in the Operation-tree and double-click the Histogram operation,
  • double-click the Histogram operation in the Operation-tree or the Operation-list,
  • in the Catalog, click a map with the right mouse button, and choose Statistics, Histogram from the context-sensitive menu,
  • drag a map from the Catalog to the Histogram operation in the Operation-tree or the Operation-list.
