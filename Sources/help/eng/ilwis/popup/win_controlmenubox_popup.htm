Control-menu box

The Control-menu box is located in the upper left corner of a window. Click the Control-menu box to open the Control menu. The Control menu is most useful if you use the keyboard (rather than the mouse) to move, size, and close a window. Double-clicking the Control-menu box of a map, table, pixel info or editor window closes that window. Double-clicking the Control-menu box of the ILWIS Main window closes ILWIS.
