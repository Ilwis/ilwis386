Table selections

Selecting fields:

  • To select a single field: click the field.
  • To select multiple adjacent fields: drag a rectangle over the fields of interest.
  • To select multiple fields out of sequence: click the first field of interest, press and hold the Shift key, and click the other fields of interest.

Selecting columns:

  • To select a single column: click a column name.
  • To select multiple consecutive columns: click the first column of interest, press and hold the Shift key, and click the last column of interest.

Selecting records:

  • To select a single record: click a record. (The record will also be shown in Record View.)
  • To select multiple consecutive records, click the first record of interest, hold the left mouse button down, move to the last record of interest and release the left mouse button.
  • To select multiple consecutive records, click the first record of interest, press and hold the Shift key, and click the last column of interest.

Selecting the contents of the whole table:

  • Open the Edit menu of the table window and choose Select All.

To clear a selection:

  • Press Esc on the keyboard.
