Conformal projections

Same angles. Angles between two directions in a map with a conformal projection and between these two directions on earth are the same, i.e. the difference in bearing (also known as the azimuth) between two points on the map and on earth is the same.

Topographic maps and maps used for navigation are usually maps with a conformal projection.
