Possible coordinate system transformations

The following coordinate system types can be transformed to each other:

  • a coordsys projection with a certain projection, ellipsoid and/or datum to another coordsys projection with a different projection, ellipsoid and/or datum;
  • a coordsys projection with a certain projection, ellipsoid and/or datum to a coordsys latlon with a certain ellipsoid and/or datum (and vice versa);
  • a coordsys latlon with a certain ellipsoid and/or datum to another coordsys latlon with a different ellipsoid and/or datum;
  • a coordsys formula to the 'related coordsys' of this coordsys formula (and vice versa);
  • a coordsys tiepoints to the 'related coordsys' of this coordsys tiepoints (and vice versa).
