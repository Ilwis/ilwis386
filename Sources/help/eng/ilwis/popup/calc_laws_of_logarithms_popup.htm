Laws of logarithms

Multiplication:

log(n*m) = log(n) + log(m)

thus log(2*3) = log(2) + log(3)

Division:

log(n/m) = log(n) - log(m)

thus log(2/3) = log(2) - log(3)

Raising to a power:

log(nm) = m * log(n)

thus log(23) = 3 * log(2)

ln(exp(n)) = n

thus ln(exp(2)) = 2

Changing the base of a logarithm

If instead of calculating 10log (a), you wish to calculate blog(a), divide the logarithm of a by the logarithm of b:

blog (a) = 10log (a) / 10log( b )
