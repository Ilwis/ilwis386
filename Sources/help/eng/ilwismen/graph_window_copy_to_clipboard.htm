Graph window

 Edit menu

Copy to clipboard

 

Copy the contents of the current graph window as a picture to the clipboard.

Subsequently, you can paste the graph into an ILWIS layout, or into any other Windows application, for instance into a word processor.

 

  • First, click the graph window displaying your graph.
  • Then, either:
    • choose the Copy command from the Edit menu, or
    • click the Copy button in the toolbar of the graph window.

You can also copy a graph by clicking in the graph window displaying your graph, and then press the Ctrl+C keys on the keyboard.

 

Tip: To copy a complete graph window including the Graph Management pane, toolbar and window borders to the clipboard, make the graph window the active window and press the Alt+PrintScrn keys on the keyboard.
