SMCE window

 Edit menu

Delete

 

Delete the currently selected item from the criteria tree.

The delete action is executed:

  • when you choose Delete from the Edit menu in a SMCE window;
  • when you click the Delete button in the toolbar of the SMCE window;
  • when you press the Delete Key on the keyboard;
  • when you click an item in the criteria tree with the right mouse button and choose Delete from the context-sensitive menu.

Notes:

  • When you delete a group of factors, also the children of that group will be deleted.
  • In the Multicriteria Analysis mode, the criteria tree structure is fixed. With a delete action, you are then only able to delete name(s) of output map(s).
