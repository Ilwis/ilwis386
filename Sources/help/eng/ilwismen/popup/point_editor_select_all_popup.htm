Select All

Selects all points in the point map.

After selecting all points, you may for instance copy all points to the clipboard. You can then paste the clipboard contents into another point map, into another editor, or into another Windows program.

 

Tip: To clear the selection, press Esc on the keyboard, or click in the white space of the editor.
