Pan

Allows you to roam through the entire page of the layout, when you zoomed in on the layout. The mouse pointer will change into a hand.

When the layout editor is in Pan mode, the Pan command is preceded by an option bullet .

Choosing the Pan command from the menu is the same as clicking the Pan button in the standard toolbar of the layout editor.

Tip:

To Pan within a selected map view on which you zoomed in, choose Pan from the context-sensitive menu.
