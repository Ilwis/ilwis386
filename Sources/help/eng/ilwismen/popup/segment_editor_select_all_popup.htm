Select All

Selects all segments in the segment map.

After selecting all segments, you may for instance copy all segments to the clipboard. You can then paste the clipboard contents into another segment map, into another editor, or into another Windows program.

 

Tip: To clear the selection, press Esc on the keyboard, or click in the white space of the editor.
