Zoom in

Zoom in on a part of this layout.

The mouse pointer changes into a magnifying glass with a large plus inside, then:

  • click somewhere in the layout, to zoom in on a certain position with a factor 2, or
  • drag a rectangle with the mouse pointer from one point of interest to another, to zoom in on the area of interest (any zoom factor).

When the layout editor window is in Zoom In mode, the Zoom In command is preceded by an option bullet .

Choosing the Zoom In command from the menu is the same as clicking the Zoom In button in the standard toolbar. You can also go to Zoom In mode by pressing the Ctrl+I keys on the keyboard.
