Zoom Out

Allows you to zoom out from a layout.

The mouse pointer changes to a 'magnifying' glass with a large minus inside, then:

  • click somewhere in your layout to zoom out with a factor 2, or
  • draw a rectangle in the layout: the smaller the rectangle, the more you will zoom out.

Choosing the Zoom Out menu command is the same as pressing the Zoom Out button in the standard toolbar. You can also zoom out by pressing the Ctrl+O keys on the keyboard.
