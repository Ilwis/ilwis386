View Angle

The View Angle is an angle in degrees which defines the aperture of your view, i.e. how much you can see of your 3D model.

  • When the view angle is small, only a small area of the total 3D view is observed, with the view point as the middle of the observed area.
  • The larger the angle, the larger the area that is observed.
  • A maximum of 180° is allowed.
