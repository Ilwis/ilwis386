Graph window

 Graph Management

X-axis (rose diagram)
 

Modify the X-axis of the rose diagram as displayed in the graph window.

Note:

The column used for the X-axis of a rose diagram is supposed to contain values between 0-180° or between 0-360°.

This dialog box appears:

  • when you double-click the X-axis item in the Graph Management pane,
  • when you double-click the X-axis as displayed below the rose diagram in the graph window itself.

Dialog box options:

Show grid:

Select this check box when you wish to have grid lines in your graph.

The grid lines will automatically be drawn for all ticks along the X-axis, i.e. as specified for Interval.

Rose size:

Specify whether the rose diagram should be displayed comprising 180° or 360°.

 180°:

To obtain a rose diagram of half a circle.

 360°:

To obtain a rose diagram of a full circle.

Interval:

Specify the interval in degrees where ticks and text should appear at the outer side of the rose diagram.

 30°:

To obtain ticks and text every 30°.

 45°:

To obtain ticks and text every 45°.

 90°:

To obtain ticks and text every 90°.
