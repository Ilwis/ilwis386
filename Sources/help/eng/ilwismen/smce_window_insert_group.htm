Insert a group

 

Insert a new group of factors under the main root of the criteria tree or under a selected group of factors in the criteria tree.

The Group dialog box appears:

  1. when you are in the Problem Definition mode, and
  2. when you selected the main goal, or when you selected a sub goal (i.e. a group), and
  3. do one of the following:
    • choose Insert Group from the Edit menu in the SMCE window,
    • click the Insert Group button in the toolbar of the SMCE window,
    • click the main root or a group of factors with the right mouse button, and choose Insert Group from the context-sensitive menu,
    • press the Insert Key on the keyboard and choose Insert Group from the context-sensitive menu.

Dialog box options:

Name:

Type a new textual name for this group of factors to describe the group.

Tips:

  • The newly inserted group will appear under the main goal or under the sub goal that you selected in step 2 above.
  • Next, add factors (benefits and costs) to the new group.
  • Optionally, specify the name of the output map that will be calculated from all factors that are added to this group.
